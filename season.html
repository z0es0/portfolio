Season

A place-based, community-sourced, local library of knowledge for people who use drugs.

Our client, a primary care physician and addiction medicine researcher at UPMC and Yale School of Medicine, tasked us with designing a "Yelp for people who use drugs."

In this exploratory project conducted through Carnegie Mellon University School of Design, my team developed the concept, wireframes, and high-fidelity prototypes for a new mobile app concept, Season - a local library of knowledge for people who use drugs.

Problem

How might we connect people who use drugs with accessible, real-time information on how to stay safe?

Outcome

I designed a mobile app that formalizes knowledge-gathering and sharing among the community of people who use drugs, and that promotes harm reduction by meeting people where they are.

DISCOVER

Season provides a comprehensive directory connecting individuals with harm reduction, treatment, and other essential services in their region. App users can search for specific needs—like access to syringes—or explore nearby resources such as housing support, counseling, and education.

The directory equips users with critical details for a successful visit, including hours, services offered, and reviews from other app users.

SHARE

The app’s review section encourages users to share their experience visiting a facility, including overall ratings, accessibility of services, and a space to share how safe, supported, and respected they felt during their visit.

TALK

The Talk section allows users to seek immediate, one-on-one support for any reason. Depending on their needs, an individual can connect with a peer navigator at a local org, call the Never Use Alone hotline to be on the phone with someone while they use drugs, or send an emergency SMS to a trusted contact.

Design System

The design system features a warm, inviting color palette and thoughtful illustrations that foster a sense of safety and community. Inspired by the four seasons, the motifs symbolize that Season is a reliable companion, available to users at every stage of their recovery journey.
